Pick the 3 cards that score the most. Click a palette card to drop it into the next empty slot, then click cards on the field to mark your pick. Used cards grey out in the palette so you can't pick the same card twice.

  • R / B / Y + 1–8 — type a card directly
  • Click a card on the field — toggle it in your pick
  • Right-click a card on the field — discard it (then enter the new card the game gave you)
  • Space — auto-select the suggested 3
  • Enter — confirm the pick
  • Backspace — undo · Esc — reset (logs the run to session)
